SPECIES

Animals

Animal species are defined as groups of organisms that can interbreed to produce fertile offspring. There are over 1.5 million known species of animals on Earth, and each one plays an important role in the ecosystem.

Plants

Plant species are defined as groups of plants that can interbreed to produce fertile offspring. There are over 380,000 known species of plants on Earth, and new species are being discovered all the time.

Learn more.....

Fungi

Fungi, also known as mushrooms, molds, and mildews, are a diverse group of eukaryotic organisms that play a vital role in the ecosystem. They are found in all parts of the world, from the tropics to the Arctic, and they thrive in a wide variety of habitats, including soil, water, and decaying matter.
